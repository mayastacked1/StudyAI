Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

At StudyAI, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by StudyAI and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal identification information (name, email address, phone number) unless you voluntarily provide it through our feedback form.

Chat Data: Your conversations are processed in real-time by our AI provider to generate responses. We do not store your chat history on our servers. All chat history is saved exclusively in your browser's local storage (LocalStorage) and never leaves your device.

How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our AI study assistant
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To respond to your feedback if you contact us

Third-Party Services

We use third-party AI services to process your questions and generate answers. These services may temporarily process your chat inputs to provide the service but do not store them long-term according to their API policies.

Local Storage

StudyAI uses LocalStorage in your browser to save your conversation history so you can revisit past chats. This data is entirely controlled by you and can be cleared at any time by deleting your chat history or clearing your browser data.
